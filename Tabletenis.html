🏓 Table Tennis

Fast-paced, thrilling, and all about precision — Let’s play!

Why Play Table Tennis?

Table Tennis enhances reflexes, coordination, and strategy — all while having a blast with friends!

Game Rules & Pro Tips

  • 🏓 First to 11 points wins, but you must lead by 2.
  • 🏓 Alternate serves every 2 points.
  • 🏓 Keep your shots fast, low, and angled for maximum control.
